## About Us

**MATEKK Cybercore Academy** is a premier ICT training and services company dedicated to building competent, job-ready technology professionals. We deliver hands-on, industry-aligned programs in networking, cybersecurity, systems administration, and a broad range of information and communications technology disciplines.

Founded on the principle that practical knowledge drives real-world performance, MATEKK bridges the gap between academic theory and the demands of modern IT infrastructure. Whether you are an individual looking to break into the tech industry or an enterprise seeking to upskill your workforce, MATEKK Cybercore Academy has a solution tailored for you.

## Training Programs

Our curriculum is structured to accommodate beginners, intermediate learners, and seasoned professionals seeking to advance or validate their expertise.

### Networking
- Network Fundamentals (OSI Model, TCP/IP, Subnetting)
- LAN/WAN Design and Implementation
- Cisco CCNA / CCNP Preparation
- Network Troubleshooting and Monitoring
- Software-Defined Networking (SDN)

### Cybersecurity
- Cybersecurity Fundamentals and Best Practices
- Ethical Hacking and Penetration Testing
- Security Operations Center (SOC) Analyst Training
- CompTIA Security+ / CEH Preparation
- Incident Response and Digital Forensics

### Systems Administration
- Windows Server Administration
- Linux System Administration (RHEL / Ubuntu)
- Active Directory and Group Policy Management
- Virtualization with VMware and Hyper-V
- Cloud Infrastructure Basics (AWS / Azure / GCP)

### IT Support & Help Desk
- CompTIA A+ and IT Technician Certification Prep
- Hardware Troubleshooting and Maintenance
- ITIL Service Desk Fundamentals
- Remote Support Tools and Ticketing Systems

### Programming & Automation
- Python for Network Automation
- Bash Scripting for System Administrators
- Introduction to DevOps Practices
- Infrastructure as Code (Ansible, Terraform)

## How to Enroll

Getting started at MATEKK Cybercore Academy is straightforward:

1. **Browse Programs** — Review our available courses and identify the program that fits your goals and current skill level.
2. **Submit an Inquiry** — Reach out via email, phone, or our website to express your interest and get detailed program information.
3. **Complete Registration** — Fill out the enrollment form and submit required documents (valid ID, educational background if applicable).
4. **Confirm Payment** — Secure your slot by completing the enrollment fee payment. Installment plans are available on request.
5. **Begin Training** — Receive your schedule, learning materials, and lab access credentials, and start your ICT journey.
